ABOUT US

Formed in 1996, Global Actives is a leading technology and professional services company. For many years we have successfully served the technology needs of several customers from diverse industries and locations throughout North America. Global Actives has a reputation for addressing its clients’ complex challenges with business understanding and innovative technical solutions. Global Actives specializes in full-lifecycle project efforts conducted in strategic partnership with some of the world’s most prominent companies.

Why Global Actives?

Many organizations have the immediate need to implement, execute and deliver on specific software engineering and IT projects, but lack the necessary resources due to budgetary or headcount constraints. However, these projects are critical to the organization and implementation cannot be delayed. Global Actives Softworld can help support those projects in multiple models.

Key Differentiators

  • Engage with clients on SOW/Project-based work and Contingent staffing (IT & Non-IT).
